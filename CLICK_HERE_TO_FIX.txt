FIX YOUR RENDER DEPLOYMENT
========================================

The compilation error you saw is already fixed in the code.
You just need to force Render to rebuild with a clean cache.

STEPS TO FIX:
========================================

1. Go to: https://dashboard.render.com

2. Click on your Web Service (backend)

3. Click "Manual Deploy" button

4. Select "Clear build cache & deploy"

5. Wait 5-10 minutes

6. Check logs for "Started SocialMessagingPlatformApplication"

7. Test: https://your-backend.onrender.com/actuator/health

IMPORTANT: Set These Environment Variables
========================================

Go to Environment tab and add:

SPRING_PROFILES_ACTIVE=prod
SPRING_DATASOURCE_URL=<get from your PostgreSQL database>
CORS_ALLOWED_ORIGINS=https://your-frontend.vercel.app

To get database URL:
- Click your PostgreSQL database
- Scroll to "Connections"
- Copy "Internal Database URL"
